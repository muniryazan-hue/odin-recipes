Cookie Salami

Go back to the list!

Description

A cookie salami out of cocoa, cookies and walnuts is my favorite childhood treat! There's so much warmth and happiness in this recipe. Today I'm making it so you all could learn to make it to with me! A sweet and aromatic salami!

Ingredients

  • Cookies 500 grams
  • Butter 200 grams
  • Walnuts or peanuts 100 grams
  • Sugar 1 glass volume 200 ml
  • Cocoa 2 full tablespoons
  • Milk 100 ml

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients for dessert. Peel the nuts.
  2. In a bowl, combine the butter, milk, sugar, and cocoa. Heat the mixture in a double boiler until all ingredients are dissolved. Do not let it boil! The main thing is for the butter to be warm and the sugar to dissolve.
  3. Using your hands or a rolling pin, break the cookies into small pieces.
  4. Grind the peeled nuts in a mortar or simply chop them with a knife. Add them to the cookies and stir.
  5. Pour sweet milk and butter over the cookies.
  6. Mix thoroughly with a spoon. It should form a thick, viscous mass.
  7. Place some of the sweet mixture on plastic wrap and roll it up tightly, like a salami. I made three small salamis. Tie the edges tightly with string to hold the salami shape, and refrigerate for 3-4 hours. After this time the salami will be ready!
