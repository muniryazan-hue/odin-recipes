Beetroot Salad

Go back to the list!

Description

Today I'll tell you how to make a beetroot salad. It's incredibly simple to prepare, but it turns out delicious. Let's get started!

Ingredients

  • Beet 4 pieces
  • Garlic 1-2 cloves
  • Mayonnaise, sour cream 1 tablespoon each
  • Salt, pepper to taste

Steps

  1. First, boil the beets and let them cool. Then peel them along with the garlic.
  2. Grate the beets, add garlic passed through a press.
  3. Season the salad with mayonnaise and sour cream, add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Cool the salad and serve. Enjoy!
