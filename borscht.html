Borscht

Description

Still not sure what to make for lunch? I want to show you an easy, step-by-step recipe for the most delicious borscht. Rich, mouth-watering, and satisfying… a wonderful idea for the whole family!

Ingredients

  • Bay leaf 1 piece
  • Mat for broth (beef, pork, chicken) 400-450 grams
  • Potato 4 pieces
  • Onion 1 piece
  • Carrot 1 piece
  • Beet 3 pieces
  • Cabbage 1/3-1/2 pieces
  • Garlic 2-3 cloves
  • Tomato paste 2 table spoons
  • Vinegar 1 tablespoon
  • Salt, pepper, vegetable oil to taste
  • Water 3-4 liters

Steps

  1. Here are the ingredients we need to prepare classic borscht with meat broth.
  2. First, you need to make meat broth. Wash the meat, place it in a saucepan, and cover with water. Add the bay leaf. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and simmer until the meat is tender (the time will depend on the type of meat you chose). Carefully remove it from the broth once it's done.
  3. Strain the broth if desired. Add salt to taste and add diced potatoes. Cook for about 10-12 minutes.
  4. Peel the onion and carrot, chop and fry in a frying pan with a little vegetable oil.
  5. Place fried vegetables and finely chopped cabbage into a saucepan with broth.
  6. Meanwhile, add a little more oil to the pan and add the grated beets. Fry for a couple of minutes and add the vinegar. Simmer for another 5 minutes, then add the tomato paste. Simmer over low heat for another 5-7 minutes.
  7. Place the meat, previously cut into portions, and beets into the broth.
  8. Add salt, pepper, sugar, and garlic to taste. Cook for another 7-10 minutes, until the potatoes are tender. Remove from heat, cover, and let sit for half an hour. Add herbs and sour cream before serving. The borscht is ready! Enjoy!
